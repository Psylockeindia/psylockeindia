What is an Off-Grid Solar System?

An off-grid solar system operates independently from the electricity grid. It stores solar energy in batteries and supplies power even during outages, making it ideal for areas with unreliable or no grid connection.

How Off-Grid Solar Works

Solar panels generate electricity during the day and charge the batteries. The stored energy is used to power your appliances at night or during power cuts, ensuring uninterrupted electricity supply.

Best Suited For

Off-grid solar systems are ideal for homes, farmhouses, hospitals, telecom towers, rural properties, and locations with frequent power cuts or no grid access.
