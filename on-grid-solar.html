What is an On-Grid Solar System?

An on-grid solar system is connected to the electricity grid and allows you to use solar power during the day while exporting excess energy to the grid through net metering. This significantly reduces your electricity bills.

How Net Metering Works

With net metering, surplus solar electricity generated during the day is exported to the grid. Your electricity meter records both import and export units, and you are billed only for the net consumption.

Best Suited For

On-grid solar systems are ideal for homes, housing societies, commercial buildings, schools, offices, and industries with reliable grid power.
